## Idea

Build a **tenantâ€“landlord management website** that helps landlords manage properties, tenants, rent, bills, and official documents in one place.

The system allows landlords to digitally maintain tenant records, track rent and utility bills, and generate official PDFs such as rent receipts, tenant verification forms, and bank-style statements.
Tenants can log in to view their rent history, bills, and download documents.

---

## Requirements / Features

- Users can sign up and log in
- Two roles: **Landlord (Admin)** and **Tenant**
- Landlords can create and manage properties
- Landlords can add tenants and assign them to properties
- Store tenant personal details and police verification info
- Landlords can add rent and utility bills (monthly)
- Track bill status (paid / unpaid)
- Generate PDFs:
  - Rent receipt
  - Tenant verification document
  - Rent/bill statement

- Tenants can view:
  - Their property details
  - Rent and bill history
  - Download generated PDFs

---

## Scope Clarification (important)

- One landlord can manage multiple properties
- One tenant belongs to one property at a time
- Tenants cannot edit data (read-only access)
